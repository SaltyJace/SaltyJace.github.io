Deer in the Forest

This render uses a small collection of trees and grass distributed accross a landscape with enough variation to still feel fresh. The deer, my hero asset, is just a simple plane with an image and warping to accentuate the lighting. The landscape is sculpted using blenders various brushes. All nature assets were sourced from polygoniq's Botaniq and distributed with their scatter system. After rendering, I used Adobe Photoshop to add a depth effect using a Z-index map
