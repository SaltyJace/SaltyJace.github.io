My Experience


3D Experience

For my 3d art workflow I use a mixture of Blender and Maya Autodesk to model assets. To texture assets I use Adobe Photoshop and Adobe 3D Substance Painter. For rendering, composition, and effects I use Unreal Engine 5.


Photo Experience

Photography has always interested me as a hobby since 2013, since then I have developed skills in composition, camera skills, and photo editing. Since then I have started my professional photography career with the University of Utah Campus Store. With 2.5 years of profesional experience and around 10 years of photography under my belt; I am fully capable to lead a team or work completely independantly.

Video Experience

I was first introduced to film in highschool and became very interested in video production and live broadcasting. Since then I have graduated with a bachelors in Film and Media Arts at the University of Utah. In my education I learned valuable film-making skills, as well as explored multiple mediums such as film, 3d, and digital video.

About Me

My Background


I am a recent graduate from the University of Utah, having completed my studies in Film & Media Arts with a minor in Games during the spring of 2023. My primary focus lies in 3D modeling, with a specialization in environment art. I possess a deep appreciation for challenging projects, constantly seeking opportunities to acquire new skills and master diverse programs. Beyond my academic and professional pursuits, my interests are outdoor activities such as hiking and rock climbing. Additionally, I am a film photography enthusiast, and consistently engage in personal 3D projects to further enhance my skills. I am currently seeking employment opportunities that align with my background and passion for 3D modeling, video editing, and photography.
